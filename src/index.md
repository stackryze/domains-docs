## Quick Start

Get your free subdomain in 4 simple steps:

1. **Sign in** with GitHub at [domain.stackryze.com](https://domain.stackryze.com/)
2. **Choose** your subdomain name (e.g., `yourname.indevs.in`)
3. **Register** instantly with default nameservers
4. **Configure** your DNS provider and update nameservers
